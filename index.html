About Us

Welcome to Srusti Academy of Management. We are dedicated to providing excellent education in management and technology...

Vision

-To become a centre of learning that excels in management education, research, consultancy and extension activities of the highest quality based on ethics, morality, and values.

Mission

-To develop professionals in various disciplines of Management Science.
-To improve quality and content of instructions to achieve academic excellence.
-To promote industry-institute partnership.
-To enhance employability through imparting industry-specific skills.
-To provide a conducive atmosphere for faculty members for improvement of teaching and research competence.
-To promote entrepreneurship spirit among students.
